About UCMH

Legacy of Excellence

Welcome to the University College of Management Hyderabad (UCMH), an integral constituent of the prestigious Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH). Founded with a vision to nurture competent management professionals, UCMH stands as a beacon of quality education and research in the field of business and administration.

Situated in the sprawling and vibrant campus at Kukatpally, our college combines deep academic rigor with real-world industry applications.
